## The Guided Experience

### Eight Modules

- Ignite CRM - The Fundraiser's Command Center
    - Complete constituent lifecycle management 360° profiles, AI-powered insights, relationship mapping, and predictive engagement scoring
- Ignite Fundraising - Every Revenue Stream. One Engine.
    - Individual, corporate, and digital philanthropy unified - opportunity management, moves management, pipeline tracking, and goal planning.
- Ignite Finance - From Donation to Financial Statement
    - Full gift processing, financial subledger, GL integration, reconciliation, and compliance - donation to journal entry in one system.
- Ignite Marketing - Personalization at Scale
    - Multi-channel orchestration - email, SMS, social - built on unified CRM data with AI-powered personalization and journey automation.
- Ignite Engagement - Events. Volunteers. Community.
    - Events, volunteers, and community groups with digital registration, scheduling, hour tracking, and constituent-facing experiences.
- Ignite Partnerships - Connected to the Giving Ecosystem
    - Multi-stakeholder platform connecting nonprofits, corporate CSR, foundations, and federated fundraisers on shared community philanthropy.
- Ignite Intelligence - Your AI-Powered Fundraising Team
    - Platform-native agentic AI delivering intelligence, guidance, task execution, and digital labor - operating 24/7 across all three layers.
- Ignite Self-Service - The Constituent's Command Center
    - Personalized constituent portal where donors view impact, manage giving, access statements, and update preferences - fully branded.
