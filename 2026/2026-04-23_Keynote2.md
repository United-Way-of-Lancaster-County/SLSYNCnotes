## Nonprofit Technology, Architecture, and AI Enablement

### Six Themes Shaping the Next 1-3 Years

1. User experience will become simpler, more diverse, and more tailored
    - Form-heavy interfaces give way to guided, conversational, role-based workflows
2. Integrated Platforms will Outperform Best-of-breed stacks
    - Separate tools fragment context
    - personalization requires shared data, shared workflows, and a shared intelligence layer
3. Horizontal CRM Vendors Remain Strong, but Vertical Specialty Wins
    - Horizontal platforms' breadth serves large orgs with deep IT
4. AI Will Move from Assistance to Action
    - Yesterday: AI Assists
    - Today: AI Creates
    - Now: AI Recommends
    - Next: AI Executes
5. Data Unification Will Determine the Quality of Personalization
    - Personalization requires a unified view
    - AI is only as goof as the data beneath it
    - A true 360° constituent model drives better retention
6. The Promise: Simplicity, Trust, and Measurable Impact
    - Simplicity
        - Consumer-grade UX
        - Configure, not customize
        - Role-based
        - Fast time to value
    - Trust
        - Transparent data model
        - AI actions that are reviewable
        - Reliable uptime and compliance
        - Vendor stability and commitment
    - Measurable Impact
        - Donor retention and upgrade rates
        - Staff productivity recovered
        - Stewardship quality and response time
        - Mission delivery efficiency
