Legacy Agency Portal vs. Partnerships in Ignite

**LAP**

- Standalone System
- Separate SQL database per org
- Limited reporting - manual exports
- Dated interface; friction for agency staff and your team
- No real-time data access

**PiI**

- Native Ignite experience - one less system to manage
- Centralized Snowflake Data Warehouse - real-time data
- Near real-time reporting with PDF/CSV export
- Modern, responsive UI consistent with Ignite
- Streamlined onboarding and user management


## v1

- Agency Onboarding
    - Self-service multi-step onboarding for agency admins - no manual setup required
- Bulk Invite Agencies
    - Admins can invite multiple partner agencies at once, dramatically reducing admin time
- Bulk Agency User Invitations
    - Agency admins invite their full team in one workflow
- Designation Matching
    - Match local designations to IRS master org records - clean data from day one
- Compliance Area
    - ACH payout eligibility, annual self-certification, and partner document tracking
- Reports Hub
    - Distribution Payee, Donor Designation, and Where Raised - with PDF/CSV export
